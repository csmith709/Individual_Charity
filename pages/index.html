About Me

My name is Charity Smith and I am a software developer with a special interest in UI/UX and website and app design. In the past I was an aesthetician and a vet assistant who worked many retail jobs as well. These past experiences give me an edge, as I have experiences in anything involving animals or the medical field, including patient booking, and experience with design, social media promotion, color theory, client satisfaction, and experience in using nearly a dozen different POS systems.


I possess an intelligent, artistic and determined mind that allows me to excel in whatever I put my mind to, and I bring this devotion to each and every project I work on so that my clients have best possible results.
